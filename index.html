Our Profile

Together We Can" is an organization that aims to strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development. Strong partnerships will contribute to environmental protection and sustainable development by mobilizing resources, sharing knowledge, promoting the creation and transfer of environmentally sound technologies, and building capacity, to support the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals in all countries, in particular developing countries.
